The Game Rules

All Players

Detectives/Suspects

Innocents Your goal is to discover the Culprit before they eliminate everyone. If you receive a Tombstone Card: You are out of the game You may not discuss the game with anyone While still alive, you may question other players to gather information. However: You cannot directly ask someone if they are the culprit. Examples of acceptable questions: “What do you usually drink at break?” “What did you have for lunch today?” “Were you on duty yesterday?” Use answers and released clues to form your suspicions.
